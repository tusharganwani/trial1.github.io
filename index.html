Welcome to Hardware Merchants Association

President's Desk

Namaskar,

Welcome to the official website of the Hardware Merchants Association, Maharashtra. Our website was launched 17 years ago and since then we have been continuously upgrading and improving it to keep it relevant and helpful.
The objective of this site is to bridge the communication gap, if any and to reach out to all the stakeholders.

Our endeavour is to give you a pleasant experience while visiting the website and to make it as informative as possible. A lot of information has been provided across the site which should be handy to the users.

If you have any query or suggestions, please feel free to contact the HMA team. We are all always grateful for feedbacks on the website and suggestions for its improvement. We believe that HMA is an association which grows stronger with the participation of all.

Do like and follow us on our offcial Facebook page for more updates:
https://www.facebook.com/hmaslg/

Warm regards,

Sri Bijay Kr. Agarwal
